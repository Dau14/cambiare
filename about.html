    I have always known that giving is a selfless act aimed at making a difference in the lives of the less fortunate. Living in a developing region like Africa, one may not need to walk very far to see the level of abject poverty and glaring disparities, which further deepen a sense of hopelessness in vulnerable souls. Cambiare, derived from the Latin word for the English word Change, surmises the reason behind establishing a Charity Organisation aimed at channelling resources for the betterment of the fragile, the less advantaged, and the poor. I have faith that as a collective driven by a selfless drive to make a difference, we can together make a sustained contribution. A contribution that will facilitate social change, welfare, and social cohesion. Founder Unarine Amandah Sikitime, Established on 2nd January 2025
    Our mission is to create a world where humans coexist peacefully with the environment. Preserving it for coming generations via preservation. Moreover to create a community in where people have access to support services and make the world a better place.
    Thru our helping hands, to sustain change, we strive to empower each individual by making them feel like they belong.
